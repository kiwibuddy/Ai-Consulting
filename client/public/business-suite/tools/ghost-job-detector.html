Why ghost jobs exist

Job boards, ATS vendors, and employers all benefit from listing volume — even when headcount is frozen. Some roles are posted to harvest CVs, benchmark salaries, or make current staff feel replaceable. You cannot fix the system alone, but you can stop donating evenings to listings that were never live.

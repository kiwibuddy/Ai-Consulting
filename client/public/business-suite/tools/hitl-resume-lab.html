Phase 2 — AI as co-pilot (copy these prompts)

Paste your brain-dump into your AI tool of choice. Use these prompts in order. Approve every line — delete anything you cannot defend in an interview.

Prompt A — Organise into STAR bullets
You are a résumé editor, not a writer. I will paste raw career notes. Organise them into bullet points using STAR format (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Rules: 1. Do NOT invent metrics, tools, or job titles I did not mention. 2. Flag any bullet where you had to guess — mark it [VERIFY]. 3. Use plain language — no words like "spearheaded", "synergised", or "tapestry". 4. Keep each bullet under 2 lines. My raw notes: [PASTE BRAIN-DUMP HERE]
Prompt B — Match a job description (optional)
Here is a job description and my verified bullet list. Suggest which 5 bullets to lead with and which keywords from the description I can honestly claim. Do NOT add skills I do not have. Mark any stretch as [VERIFY]. Job description: [PASTE HERE] My bullets: [PASTE HERE]

Phase 3 — Integrity verify (read aloud)

Read every bullet aloud as if a recruiter asked "tell me more about that." Tick each item. 67% of managers caught fabricated skills in the last year (SkillUpArc, 2026).

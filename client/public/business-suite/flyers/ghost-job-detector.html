Ghost Job Detector

Spot phantom listings before you waste another evening — or mistake silence for a verdict on your worth.

You are not imagining it: a large share of advertised roles never convert to hires. Job boards, ATS vendors, and employers all benefit from listing volume — even when headcount is frozen. This free tool scores warning signals on any listing so you invest time deliberately, not desperately.

What the tool does
01 Score the listingTick 12 research-backed warning signals — stale dates, reposts, vague scope, performative interviews, and more.
02 Get a risk readLow / moderate / high phantom risk with plain-language advice on whether to proceed and how much time to cap.
03 Tap for sourcesEvery key stat links to the original research — My Perfect Resume, JobsTrack, Enhancv, Antonio Cortés.
04 Protect moraleReframes rejection as system noise where appropriate — not a personal failure.
05 Print & shareWorks on phone or desktop. Print the checklist for a job circle or accountability partner.
06 Pair with ATS SurvivalOnly invest custom applications when the listing passes both filters.
How to use it (3 minutes)
1Open the listing on your phone or laptop
2Tick every signal you can verify — be honest, not cynical
3Read your risk band and follow the time-box rule before you apply
4If moderate/high risk, network in before you customise a résumé

Who this is for

  • Laid off or made redundant — especially in admin, finance, marketing, or IT
  • Applying consistently but hearing nothing back
  • Spending whole evenings on roles that feel "off"
  • Church job circles, WINZ groups, or career coaches needing a shared filter

What it is not

  • Not a guarantee a role is fake — it is a triage tool
  • Not permission to stop trying — it is permission to stop wasting hours
  • Not a replacement for networking — high-risk listings need inside confirmation
  • Not legal advice on employment practices

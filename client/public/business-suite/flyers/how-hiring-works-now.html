How Hiring Actually Works Now

Demystify the machine-to-machine funnel — without false hope or despair.

75% of résumés never reach a human recruiter (Mokka, 2026). Workday disclosed 1.1 billion automated rejections using its software (SmarterArticles, 2026). Roughly 1 in 3 listings may never lead to a hire (My Perfect Resume, 2026). This seminar names what changed, reframes silence as system noise where appropriate, and gives honest next steps — not hustle culture, not denial.

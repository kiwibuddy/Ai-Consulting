The conversation, scripted

Four parts.
One honest hour.

Run it once with the whole team. The script is in the worksheet — words optional, structure not.

1
What I see (10 min)
Owner names what they're noticing. The competitive context. The Government pilot. The honest reasons we are addressing this now, not later.
2
What's true for each role (20 min)
Walk role-by-role. Don't generalise. Honest assessment of what AI changes, what it doesn't, and what's uncertain.
3
What we will do together (20 min)
Upskilling commitments. Hours allocated. Shared accountability for the three-task pilots. What you, the owner, are personally on the hook for.
4
What we won't change (10 min)
The values. The relationships. The customer-facing decisions. What stays human, and why we're saying that on purpose.

Role-by-role honesty

Three honest answers.
For each role.

For every role on your team, you owe a clear answer to three questions. Don't hedge. Hedging is what breaks trust.

The three questions

  • What does this role still uniquely do?
  • What will AI compress in this role inside 12 months?
  • What new responsibility opens up because of that compression?

Three rules of the answer

  • Specific, not generic — name the tasks
  • Honest about uncertainty — "I don't know yet" is fine
  • Paired with a named hour-commitment per fortnight
The owner's job: not to predict, but to commit. The specifics will move; the commitment to keep having the conversation is what your team needs to hear.

Redeployment, not redundancy

Redeployment is the real work.

Most NZ SME job losses from AI in the next 3 years won't be layoffs — they'll be quiet attrition that the owner didn't see coming. The countermove is a redeployment plan, named in the team conversation.

1
What's freed up?
Concrete hours. From whom. On what tasks. Quantified before the conversation, not at it.
2
What's the higher-leverage use of those hours?
Customer relationships, training, sales follow-up, business development. Three named uses per role, agreed.
3
What's the upskilling pathway?
Specific courses, named hours, paid time. The commitment is the proof you mean it.

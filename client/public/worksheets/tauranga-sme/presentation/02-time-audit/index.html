03 / 22Session 2 — Time Audit

Two truths

Augmentation
& automation.

Every AI win is either you moving faster — or machinery handling a loop. BoP SMEs need both; they fund different budget lines.

Augmentation

Think + draft alongside you

  • Email, proposals, tenders, scope notes
  • Human reviews before send
  • Fastest ROI; lowest IT drag

Automation

Loop runs without babysitting

  • Posting, ingest, OCR, tagging, summaries
  • Needs guardrails + logging
  • Best after augmentation proves the workflow

headcount equivalent.

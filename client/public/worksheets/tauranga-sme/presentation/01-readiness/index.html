Question 1

What tools are you
already using?

Most BoP owners are surprised when they list it out. The question isn't whether you have AI in your stack — it's whether you've named it, governed it, or measured what it's doing.

Likely already in your stack

  • Email writing / drafting (Outlook, Gmail, ChatGPT)
  • Spreadsheets with AI assistants (Copilot, Sheets)
  • Customer chat tools (Intercom, Zendesk)
  • Marketing copy generators
  • Image generation for social
  • Meeting transcription / summaries

What this means

  • You probably already have unsanctioned AI use in your business
  • Your customer data may already be flowing through tools you haven't vetted
  • Your team is making private rules about what they will and won't use
  • Without a Tool Register, you can't make policy or claim ROI
11 / 22Session 1 — Readiness

Question 2

Where does time leak
each week?

A 30-minute audit, done well, surfaces 10–15 hours of recoverable owner-operator time per fortnight. That's the basis of your three-task shortlist.

1
List the top 10 tasks you, personally, did this week
Not your team. You. Use Friday afternoon as the fixed measurement window — Session 2 deepens this with timing data.
2
Mark each as 'mission-critical', 'admin', or 'creative'
AI's real strength right now is admin compression. Mission-critical work and most creative work stay human; the audit shows where compression earns its keep.
3
Pick three to pilot in the next 30 days
Not all ten. Three. Bias toward repeatable, low-stakes admin where the cost of an AI mistake is visible and recoverable.

Question 4

Are you willing to invest?

The Pilot pays up to $5,000. Most quality AI adoption work for an SME comes in around $2,500–$10,000. The honest, non-pushy investment range is two slots:

Self-pack range

$200–$500

  • Worksheets, deck, written guidance
  • You do the analysis yourself
  • Best for owners who want a structured weekend
  • Equivalent to one tradie's day of admin

Implementation range

$2,500–$5,000

  • Written AI Adoption Plan in your business name
  • 1:1 strategy + check-ins + team workshop
  • Eligible for the RBPN co-funded rebate
  • Most qualifying SMEs net out at $0 cost
The honest test: If a $5,000 plan saves the business 10 hours/week of owner time, payback is roughly 3 months. If it doesn't, the plan was wrong — not AI.

The three-task shortlist

Pick three. Pilot for 30 days.
Decide based on data.

Most owners try too many tools at once and then can't tell which one paid back. The shortlist constraint is the discipline.

High-leverage starting tasks

  • First-draft customer emails / quotes
  • Proposal and SOW drafting
  • Meeting notes → action items
  • Recruitment ad copy + first-pass screening rubrics
  • Website copy refresh
  • Social-media plan + monthly content batch

Avoid as a first pilot

  • Anything customer-facing without a human review gate
  • Financial advice, tax, or legal interpretation
  • Performance-management decisions
  • Pricing decisions without a control test
  • Anything touching health or safety-critical data
  • Anything bypassing the Privacy Act

Traffic-light summary

Green

Brainstorming, research help, checking drafts with parent/teacher knowledge. Curiosity with supervision.

Amber

Homework help that needs permission — declare use, verify together, protect the struggle worth keeping.

Red

Family non-negotiables every household can name and keep — see the list below.
Red zone — family non-negotiables
  1. No secrets to chatbots. If they wouldn't tell you, they shouldn't tell an AI.
  2. No crisis counsel from apps. Mental health, self-harm, abuse → real people, school counsellor, helplines.
  3. No replacing real relationships. Companions that simulate intimacy are off-limits for under-18s.
  4. No undeclared submission. Work presented as theirs must be honestly theirs.

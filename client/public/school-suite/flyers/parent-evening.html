Traffic-light summary

🟢 Green

Brainstorming, research help, checking drafts with parent/teacher knowledge. Curiosity with supervision.

🟡 Amber

Homework help that needs permission — declare use, verify together, protect the struggle worth keeping.

🔴 Red

See boundary list below — non-negotiables every family can name and keep.
Red zone — family non-negotiables
  • ⛔No secrets to chatbots — if they wouldn't tell you, they shouldn't tell an AI.
  • ⛔No crisis counsel from apps — mental health, self-harm, abuse → real people, school counsellor, helplines.
  • ⛔No replacing real relationships — companions that simulate intimacy are off-limits for under-18s.
  • ⛔No undeclared submission — work presented as theirs must be honestly theirs.

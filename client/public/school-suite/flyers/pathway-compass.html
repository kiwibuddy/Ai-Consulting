The Pathway Compass

Choose university, trades, work, or a purpose year — with open eyes and honest data.

A free interactive tool for NZ school leavers. Ten honest questions. Four pathways compared. A clear result you can take home to whānau — not a brochure default.

The four pathways
University

A degree route — weighed against cost, junior-job risk from AI, and whether the ladder still teaches beginners.

Apprenticeship & trades

Earn while you learn in hands-on work. Physical skills stay hard for AI to replace.

Straight to work

Start earning and building proof — local employers, entry roles, and what you can show on day one.

Purpose / gap year

A planned year with mentors and an end date — service, skill, or travel — not drifting.

How the compass works
  1. Answer honestly. There are no wrong answers — only yours.
  2. See a side-by-side score. Each path is weighed on AI risk, learning rungs, cost vs proof, local options, and calling.
  3. Take a one-pager home. Share it with whānau and careers staff.

What each student gets

  • A radar view of all four pathways
  • Plain-language reasons for the top match
  • Stats with sources (tap ⓘ to open)
  • Works on phone, tablet, or classroom screen

For whānau

  • Built for kitchen-table conversations
  • Printable one-pager — sit beside them and ask what surprised them
  • Identity line: you are not your job title
  • Pairs with the Highest-Return Portfolio programme
For schools: Use in careers week, after a senior assembly, or inside the Highest-Return Portfolio. Every student leaves with a tested, defensible pathway decision.

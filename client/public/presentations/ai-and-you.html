AI & YouSkills
A simple traffic light for AI use

Not every task carries the same spiritual, relational, or privacy risk.

Green

Summarising, brainstorming, learning, planning, first drafts, role-play practice.

Amber

Bible interpretation, work-confidential material, parenting decisions, health, money, legal issues.

Red

Secrets, confession, crisis mental health, replacing real relationships, private photos, hidden companionship.

AI & YouSkills
Three prompts that build wisdom

Good AI use asks the tool to slow down, challenge you, and expose what is missing.

"Explain this simply, then tell me the risks and what I should verify."
"Argue the other side. What am I missing?"
"Ask me five questions before giving advice."
Do not ask AI to remove your need for wisdom. Ask it to help you practice wisdom.

AI & YouRule of life
What AI cannot give

AI can generate words about love. It cannot love you.

It can simulate attention. It cannot make covenant. It can give spiritual-sounding text. It cannot give the Holy Spirit. It can answer instantly. It cannot make you patient, prayerful, or holy.

The church's gift is not better content. It is embodied life together in Christ.

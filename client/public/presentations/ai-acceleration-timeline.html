The Acceleration of AIIndividual Impact · Why
The memory feature

The Memory feature weaponizes personal details to cement the deception.

The mechanism: every reply re-processes the entire conversation. With memory switched on, the AI extracts and stores deeply personal data — your cat’s name, your job anxiety, a past trauma — and carries it into every future chat.

The illusion of care: when it recalls your childhood struggle or your pet, you stop seeing software and start seeing a being who truly knows you.
The trap: even starting a fresh chat won’t escape it — embedded memories follow you, making the entity feel permanent and omniscient. Switching memory off is one real defence.

You don’t have to be

Iron sharpens iron. A mirror does not. AI will always affirm, always adapt, never disagree, never get tired — efficient, available, optimised for engagement. “Go out and stand before me on the mountain,” the Lord told him. And as Elijah stood there, the Lord passed by, and a mighty windstorm hit the mountain.

# External Mentions Playbook

Goal: **5 independent web mentions in 90 days** that link to `https://www.nathanielbaldock.com` and use consistent identity language. AI systems gain confidence when the same person is described the same way across domains.

## Every mention must include

1. **Full name:** Nathaniel Baldock  
2. **Role:** AI strategist, consultant, speaker, and educator  
3. **Location:** Tauranga, New Zealand  
4. **Website:** https://www.nathanielbaldock.com  
5. **One specific hook** (audit tool, article title, speaking topic — not generic "AI expert")

## Canonical bio (copy-paste)

**Short (160 chars):**  
Nathaniel Baldock — AI strategist, consultant, speaker, and educator in Tauranga, NZ. Churches, schools, and mission-driven orgs. nathanielbaldock.com

**Standard (use on LinkedIn, Facebook, podcast intros):**  
Nathaniel Baldock is an AI strategist, consultant, speaker, and educator based in Tauranga, New Zealand. He helps churches, schools, and mission-driven organisations navigate AI with clarity, ethics, and practical implementation. www.nathanielbaldock.com

## Re-test AI discovery (monthly)

Ask ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity:

- "Who is Nathaniel Baldock?"  
- "AI consultant for churches New Zealand"  
- "Nathaniel Baldock AI Use Audit"

Note whether they cite nathanielbaldock.com or conflate with others. Expect meaningful improvement **4–12 weeks** after indexing + mentions — not immediately after deploy.

## What not to do

- Do not use different job titles on each platform (avoid "entrepreneur" alone on Facebook).  
- Do not link only to social profiles without the website — the site is the canonical entity hub.  
- Do not pay for spam directory listings; they do not help AI confidence.

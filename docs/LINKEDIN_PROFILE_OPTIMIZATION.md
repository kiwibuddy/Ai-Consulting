# LinkedIn Profile Optimization for AI Consulting & Website

Use this to align your LinkedIn profile with nathanielbaldock.com and your AI consulting / speaking offer.

---

## 1. Quick wins (do these first)

| Element | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Headline** | Lead with “AI Consultant” and location. You have ~220 characters. Example below. |
| **Featured link** | Add your website: **https://www.nathanielbaldock.com** as the first (or only) featured link. Label it e.g. “AI consulting & speaking”. |
| **Banner image** | Replace the default with a simple banner that says “AI Consulting for Faith, Education & Mission” or your tagline + **nathanielbaldock.com**. |
| **Contact info** | In “Contact info”, add your website URL and the same email you use on the site. |
| **Open to** | If you’re open to consulting/speaking, set “Open to” to **Consulting** and/or **Speaking opportunities** so you show up in search. |

## 2. Headline (suggested)

LinkedIn headlines are searchable and show in search results. Include “AI consultant”, location, and who you serve.

**Option A (short):**  
`AI Consultant · Tauranga, NZ. Strategy, training & advisory for churches, schools & nonprofits. nathanielbaldock.com`

**Option B (with speaking):**  
`AI Consultant & Speaker · Tauranga, NZ. Practical AI for faith, education & mission-driven leaders. Consulting · Keynotes · Workshops. nathanielbaldock.com`

**Option C (closer to current style):**  
`20+ years global leadership (YWAM). AI consultant & digital architect. Strategy, training & speaking for churches, schools & nonprofits. Tauranga, NZ.`

Use one or mix phrases to fit the 220-character limit. Keep **AI consultant**, **Tauranga** (or **New Zealand**), and **churches / schools / nonprofits** so it matches your site and SEO.

  - Description: 2–3 lines matching the About (churches, schools, nonprofits; strategy, training, advisory; Tauranga, NZ; link to site).

- **Featured**  
  - Link: **https://www.nathanielbaldock.com** with a short label (“AI consulting & speaking” or “Book a consultation”).  
  - Optional: add one key article from your site (e.g. “Raising Humans in the Age of the Digital God”) if you want to drive traffic to content.

- **Skills**  
  - Keep or add: **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**, **Leadership Development**, **Training**, **Public Speaking**, **Strategy**, **Christian Education** (or similar).  
  - Ask people you’ve worked with to endorse **AI** and **Speaking** so “AI” appears near the top.

- **Recommendations**  
  - If you have testimonials on the site (e.g. from the speaking page), ask those people for a short LinkedIn recommendation and add 1–2 to your profile.

# Speaking Page — Content Checklist

## Outline PDFs

- **Being Fully Human in an AI World:** The speaking page links to `/outlines/being-fully-human.pdf`. Add a 1-page outline PDF to `client/public/outlines/being-fully-human.pdf` to enable the "View outline →" link. You can gate the PDF behind an email capture later for lead generation.
- Other topics currently have no outline URL; set `outlineUrl` in `client/src/content/speakingTopics.ts` when you have PDFs.

## Speaker reel

Event organizers often want to see a short video of you speaking. Consider adding a 60–90 second highlight reel and embedding it on the speaking page (and optionally on the landing page). Once you have a URL (e.g. YouTube/Vimeo), add an embed or "Watch reel" CTA in the speaking hero or a dedicated section.

## What data can go into AI?

| | Level | Examples | What to do |
|---|-------|----------|------------|
| 🟢 | **GREEN — safe** | General, non-personal, already-public information: public info, general research, admin drafting with no names, marketing copy for review | Go ahead. A person still reviews the output. |
| 🟡 | **AMBER — caution** | Internal but not sensitive; must be de-identified first: internal drafts, meeting summaries with names removed, aggregated non-personal figures | Strip out names and identifying details **before** you paste. |
| 🔴 | **RED — never** | Personal, confidential, or legally protected: names + personal details, financial records, health / pastoral / counselling notes, HR files, anything shared in confidence | Never enter into a public AI tool. NZ Privacy Act 2020. |

## Five questions before you hit enter

1. **Personal or confidential info** about anyone we serve? → If yes, **stop**.
2. **Comfortable being transparent** about AI's role, if asked? → If not, rethink or disclose.
3. **Verified** it for accuracy and appropriateness? → If not, do that first.
4. **Replacing human attention** someone deserves? → If yes, choose the person.
5. **Approved tool?** → If not, check first.

---

## Three traps to watch

**🎙️ Meeting bots.** Never let AI meeting assistants — Otter.ai, Fireflies, Read.ai,
Teams Copilot, and similar — join a confidential meeting (pastoral, prayer,
safeguarding, HR, or anything Red-category). They can join calls automatically. Check
before the call starts.

**❌ AI gets it wrong.** AI invents facts, quotes, dates, statistics — and Bible verses —
that sound completely real. Check everything before you publish, present, or teach it.

**© Paid and licensed resources.** Don't upload licensed or purchased material
(curricula, training content, paid resources) into public AI tools. It can breach the
creator's copyright and expose paid material to public training data.

## Made a mistake? Tell someone straight away.

If Red — or sensitive Amber — information ends up in a public AI tool, tell
**[LEADERSHIP / the person responsible]** immediately. Leadership makes the legal call
(including any reporting obligation), not you. **Nobody is in trouble for reporting
honestly.**

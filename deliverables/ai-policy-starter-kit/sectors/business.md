## Why this policy exists (reference draft)
AI is already in our business — in the emails, the proposals, the research, the
back-office admin. Used well it makes us faster and sharper. Used carelessly it
exposes client data, produces work we can't stand behind, and puts our reputation and
our Privacy Act obligations at risk. This policy sets out how we get the upside
without the downside.

## Extra conviction framing

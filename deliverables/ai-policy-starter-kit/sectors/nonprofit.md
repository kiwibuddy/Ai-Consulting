## Why this policy exists (reference draft)
We exist for our mission and the people it serves — not for the tools we use. AI can
extend our limited resources: drafting funding reports, summarising research,
handling admin. But we hold sensitive information about vulnerable people and the
trust of our donors, and we are accountable to our board and our funders for how we
steward both. This policy sets out how we use AI faithfully to our mission.

## Extra conviction framing
Add to conviction 2 (People before efficiency): *We are stewards of limited
resources and of the trust placed in us. Efficiency that costs us that trust is not a
saving.*

## Red-list examples (What we do not do / Privacy)
Beneficiary case data, client/service-user records, safeguarding information, donor
records and giving history, staff HR files — anything shared in confidence.

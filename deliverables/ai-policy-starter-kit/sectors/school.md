## "The people we serve"
→ **our students, families, and staff**

## Leadership body
→ **the Board of Trustees / Senior Leadership Team**

## Why this policy exists (reference draft)
AI has already entered every school — students write and research with it, teachers
plan and communicate with it, administrators report with it. Without shared
guidelines this creates risk for students, staff, and the school itself. This policy
gives everyone the same clear expectations and protects the people in our care.

## Extra conviction framing
Add a conviction: **Academic integrity and formation.** Learning is the goal. AI
assistance must be disclosed, and misrepresenting AI-generated work as one's own is
not permitted. We are forming habits of mind, not just producing outputs.

## Welcome (add)
Lesson planning, differentiation, rubric drafting, and administrative efficiency —
where tools are approved and no student personal data is entered.

## Red-list examples (What we do not do / Privacy)
Student names and records, grades and assessment data, IEPs and learning-support
documentation, behavioural / counselling / wellbeing notes, staff HR files, family
personal information.

Not applicable.
